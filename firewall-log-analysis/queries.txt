# Scenario 1 – Port Scanning Detection

## Port Scan Summary

| stats count by src_ip,dest_ip,dest_port,action

## Top Source IPs

| stats count by src_ip
| sort -count

## Top Destination Ports

| stats count by dest_port
| sort -count

## Allowed vs Denied

| stats count by action
